Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

EatGetHealthy is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Uploaded Documents: Health-related documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT) that you voluntarily upload for processing. These are used to build the knowledge base.
  • Conversation Data: Questions you ask and the AI-generated responses, stored in session memory to enable follow-up questions and contextual conversation.
  • Technical Data: Server logs that may include IP addresses, browser type, and access timestamps for security and operational purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide and improve the service:

  • Document Processing: Uploaded documents are chunked, embedded, and stored in our database to enable AI-powered search and question answering.
  • Conversation Memory: Your chat history is stored in memory (up to 2 GB) to enable contextual follow-up questions within a session.
  • Knowledge Graph: Entities and relationships are extracted from documents to build a structured knowledge graph for enhanced retrieval.

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or selling to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on our servers with the following protections:

  • All connections are encrypted via TLS/SSL (HTTPS).
  • Document data and knowledge graphs are stored in a Neo4j database on our server.
  • Conversation memory is stored in application memory and persisted to the database.
  • We implement standard security practices including firewalls, access controls, and regular updates.

4. Third-Party Services

EatGetHealthy uses the following third-party services to provide its functionality:

  • Google Gemini API: Used for AI response generation, entity extraction, and document summarization. Your queries and document excerpts are sent to Google's servers for processing. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed by their API.
  • Sentence Transformers: Embedding models run locally on our server. No data is sent to external services for embedding generation.

5. Data Retention

  • Documents: Processed document data remains in the knowledge base until you explicitly delete it through the application interface.
  • Conversations: Session data is retained in memory during active use and persisted to the database. You can clear individual sessions or all conversation history at any time.
  • Server Logs: Technical logs are retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging purposes.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your data: Clear all document data and conversation history through the application interface.
  • Access your data: View your conversation history and uploaded document information through the application.
  • Control uploads: Choose what documents to upload and when to remove them.

7. Children's Privacy

EatGetHealthy is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Cookies

The application may use session cookies to maintain your interaction state. We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through the information provided on the website.
